About Dr. Arneja

I am Dr. Govindpreet Singh Arneja (B.P.T., M.I.A.P.). My career in physiotherapy spans more than two decades, during which I have helped thousands of patients regain their independence through evidence-based practice.

I specialize in Orthopedic and Neurological rehabilitation, utilizing advanced techniques like Manual Therapy, PNF (Proprioceptive Neuromuscular Facilitation), and detailed Gait Analysis. My approach combines clinical precision with compassionate care, ensuring that every patient understands their condition and their path to healing.

Whether you are dealing with chronic back pain, recovering from a sports injury, or requiring post-surgical rehabilitation, I am dedicated to providing a professional, calm environment where your health is the only priority.
