Is Your Back Aching? Simple Stretches to Relieve Lower Back Pain

Lower back pain affects millions worldwide, often caused by long hours of sitting, poor posture, or sudden strain. At Mehar Physiotherapy Clinic, I believe in a proactive approach combining gentle movement, strengthening, and posture correction to keep your back strong.

Effective Habits to Reduce Lower Back Pain

  • Incorporate light daily stretches to release tension in the lower back.
  • Maintain good sitting posture and use supportive seating.
  • Strengthen core muscles to protect the spine from future strain.

Consistent, gentle movement can help reduce pain and prevent recurrence. For personalized guidance and hands-on care, I can create a tailored plan that suits your needs.
