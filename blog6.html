Staying Active and Independent: How Physiotherapy Helps Seniors

Posted on August 18, 2025

Staying active is essential for older adults to maintain strength, balance, and overall quality of life. At Mehar Physiotherapy Clinic, we focus on helping seniors move confidently, reduce pain, and prevent injuries.

Key Habits and Tips for Seniors

  • Improved balance to reduce fall risk.
  • Pain management strategies for arthritis and other chronic conditions.
  • Safe recovery after surgeries like knee or hip replacements.
  • Strength and endurance training to support everyday activities.
  • Maintaining independence through improved mobility and self-confidence.

With the right care, aging doesn’t have to mean slowing down. Our physiotherapists can create programs that keep you active and enjoying life.
